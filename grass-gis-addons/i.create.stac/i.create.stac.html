Description

i.create.stac Creates a STAC item with all parsed raster layers as STAC assets. Adds timestamp based on Sentinel-2 ID. Adds items to given STAC collection and catalog.

EXAMPLES

i.create.stac assets_paths="data/ndvi.tif,data/ndwi.tif" assets_names="NDVI,NDWI" s2_id="S2A_20210101T000000" stac_id_prefix=s2_indices stac_title="Sentinel-2 NDVI and NDWI" stac_description="Sentinel-2 NDVI and NDWI indices ..." stac_catalog="stac/catalog.json" stac_collection=s2_indices \
